What We Do

Our Core Services

Methodologically rigorous and contextually grounded — our work meets the highest standards of German development cooperation and international evaluation practice.

01 ——

Project Monitoring Systems

Design and operationalisation of MEAL frameworks, indicator matrices, and real-time dashboards aligned with BMZ, KfW, and GIZ standards.

02 ——

Programme Evaluations

Independent mid-term and final evaluations applying OECD-DAC criteria — relevance, coherence, effectiveness, efficiency, impact, and sustainability.

03 ——

Baseline & Endline Studies

Mixed-methods data collection and statistical analysis establishing robust evidence baselines in fragile and complex country contexts.

04 ——

Theory of Change Development

Facilitated co-creation with programme teams to build credible, evidence-based theories of change and logical frameworks.

05 ——

Impact Assessments

Rigorous quasi-experimental evaluations using DiD, RDD, and PSM approaches for credible attribution in development programmes.

06 ——

Capacity Development

Training partner institutions in M&E best practices, data literacy, and results-based management — delivered in German, English, and French.

How We Work

Our Methodology

01

Inception & Co-Design

Deep immersion in programme logic, evaluability assessment, and stakeholder mapping. Every engagement begins with a rigorous inception phase before data collection.

02

Mixed-Methods Collection

Quantitative surveys, key informant interviews, focus groups, and observational work. Mobile tools (KoBoToolbox, ODK) and triangulated data for robustness.

03

Rigorous Analysis

Statistical modelling (Stata, R), qualitative coding (ATLAS.ti), contribution analysis, and process tracing — all subject to internal peer review.

04

Learning & Uptake

Results workshops, management response facilitation, and knowledge products tailored for technical and executive audiences — in German and English as standard.
