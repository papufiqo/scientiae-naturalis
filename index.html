Scientiae
Naturalis High-Stakes Intelligence for Complex Systems

Scientiae Naturalis is a Berlin-based professional and scientific advisory firm specialising in High-Stakes Knowledge Arbitrage. We operate at the intersection of natural science, policy, data systems, and institutional capacity — translating rigorous research into decision-grade intelligence for governments, international organisations, and private-sector clients.

Our work spans four strategic industries: Health, Food, Environment, and Education. Across these domains, we design, implement, and evaluate complex programmes where the cost of poor information is institutional, economic, or humanitarian.

An R&D enterprise founded by university professors and spin-off founders in Germany. We operate at the intersection of research, consultancy, and knowledge brokerage — converting domain-specific scientific intelligence into high-value decisions for enterprise clients.

Health Industry

Arbitrage Domain

Health product policy, immunisation and vaccine intelligence, regulatory prequalification, digital health infrastructure, health emergency risk assessment, and pharmaceutical norms and standards advisory. We translate epidemiological and regulatory data into procurement-grade decision support for ministries of health and multilateral agencies.

Food Industry

Arbitrage Domain

Food and nutrition systems, agri-food economics and policy, food standards and zoonotic disease monitoring, nutrition and food safety, and supply chain traceability. We operate commodity and price intelligence platforms and early warning systems for food security crises at regional and global scale.

Environment Industry

Arbitrage Domain

Climate-agriculture data systems, natural resource management, environmental impact and strategic assessments, biodiversity monitoring, remote sensing analytics, and renewable energy systems. We build and operate data platforms for climate risk modelling, ESG reporting, and sustainable production streams.

Education Industry

Arbitrage Domain

Education management information systems (EMIS), policy advisory, teacher training and workforce management, digital learning infrastructure, inclusive and gender-responsive education design, TVET alignment, and large-scale programme implementation for Erasmus+, UNESCO, and World Bank frameworks. Full-cycle delivery from strategy to evaluation.

SDG Alignment

Framework

All service lines are structured around verifiable SDG 4 (quality education), SDG 2 (zero hunger), SDG 3 (good health), and SDG 13 (climate action) outcomes. We provide benchmarking, indicator monitoring, and cross-country comparability frameworks aligned to PISA-equivalent international assessment systems.

Programme Outsourcing

Implementation

Full outsourcing of multi-country project coordination, grant writing and proposal engineering, KPI dashboard systems, impact assessment, and independent project evaluation of EACEA-type programmes. We assume complete operational and reporting responsibility for consortium-led projects across jurisdictions.
